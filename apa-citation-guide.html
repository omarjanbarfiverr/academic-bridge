APA Citation

APA citation basics for academic reports

A student-friendly overview for keeping sources clear, consistent, and easier to verify.

What APA citations do

APA citations help readers identify where ideas, data, and quotations come from. A complete report usually needs both in-text citations and a reference list.

In-text citations

In-text citations usually include the author's name and year. Direct quotations also need page or paragraph information where available.

Reference list consistency

  • Every cited source should appear in the reference list.
  • Every reference list entry should be cited in the text.
  • Author names, years, titles, journal names, links, and formatting should stay consistent.

Common mistakes

Students often mix citation styles, forget retrieval information, cite sources not listed in references, or list references that never appear in the report.
