Privacy Policy

This page explains how inquiry data is collected and used.

Data collected

Forms may collect name, email, phone number, academic level, school, project type, subject, deadline, language, and project question.

Purpose

Data is used to respond to inquiries and help visitors continue the project discussion through Fiverr. This website does not process payments.

Third parties

Visitors are redirected to Fiverr for paid communication, ordering, delivery, revisions, and payment. Analytics or cookie tools may be added later with appropriate notice.

Retention

Inquiry data should be retained only as long as needed for follow-up, normally 6 to 12 months unless a longer period is legally required.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your inquiry data by contacting the site owner.
