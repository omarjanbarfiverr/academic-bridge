FAQ

Questions about PFE, TFE, research support and Fiverr ordering

Clear answers before you send your project details.

Is payment secure?

Yes. This website does not process payments. Fiverr handles paid orders, communication, payment, delivery, and revisions.

Do you complete academic work for students?

No. Support is limited to ethical academic assistance such as formatting, proofreading, citation guidance, research planning, methodology guidance, structure improvement, and educational support.

Do you support PFE and TFE projects?

Yes. Support may include structure planning, formatting, proofreading, citations, methodology guidance, literature review organization, and presentation support.

What academic levels do you assist with?

Support may be available for undergraduate, final-year, master, engineering, business, and professional academic reports depending on scope and academic integrity requirements.

What languages do you support?

English, French, and bilingual English/French projects.

Can I request revisions?

Yes. Revisions are handled through Fiverr according to the agreed order scope.

Do you guarantee grades?

No. No ethical academic support service can guarantee grades, supervisor approval, or academic outcomes.

What formatting styles are supported?

Common academic formatting styles can be supported, including APA citations, university templates, page layout, tables, figures, references, and appendices.

How fast is delivery?

Delivery depends on scope, page count, complexity, language, and deadline. Timelines are discussed through Fiverr before an order begins.
