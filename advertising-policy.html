Advertising Policy

How this website handles advertising, sponsored content, and Fiverr redirects.

Advertising

This website may display advertising in the future, including Google AdSense, if approved. Ads should not be placed in a way that misleads visitors or interferes with navigation.

Fiverr redirects

Buttons that mention Fiverr redirect visitors to Fiverr for paid communication, ordering, delivery, payment, and revisions. This website does not process payments directly.

Sponsored content

Sponsored content, if ever published, should be disclosed clearly and must respect academic integrity and advertising policies.

Ad placement standards

Ads should not appear on thin, misleading, adult, violent, hateful, or dishonest-behavior content. Academic content should remain educational and policy-safe.
