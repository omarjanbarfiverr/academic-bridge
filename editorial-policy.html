Editorial Policy

How educational content is created, reviewed, and updated.

Content purpose

Articles are created to educate students about academic structure, citations, formatting, methodology, productivity, and responsible project planning.

Academic integrity

Content must not encourage cheating, ghostwriting, exam completion, fake credentials, or submission of work that students are expected to complete independently.

Originality

Articles should be original, useful, and written for students. Sources and concepts should be explained clearly, and copied content should not be published.

Corrections and updates

Articles should be reviewed periodically for accuracy, clarity, and policy safety. Errors should be corrected when discovered.
